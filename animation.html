This is a little personal project of mine that is based off a story premise I've been working on, testing my knowledge and skills aswell as giving me something fun to do.

With a background in cognitive science, his expertise in sound engineering, and any spare pieces of electrical tat found in his electonics repair shop, Dr. Ivan Grokinski (Dr. Grok) builds 'The Dream Catcher', a super computer that attempts to emulate the human brain using only specific sound frequencies to traffic information between mind and machine. Using himself as the only test subject, Dr. Grok slowly begins to succumb to the pleasentries of his own creation. As he becomes more and more secluded away from reality, will he ever break free from his own dream?

Music - 'High Score Theme' from the game 'Battle Squadron', Composed by Ron Klaren
