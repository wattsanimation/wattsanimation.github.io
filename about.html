About this Website

Initially I just wanted to publish a new portfolio site, but my curiosity grew, and with some guidence on on the side I eventually started to learn and develop my own site in HTML/CSS.

Now this site has also become an experiment in self sustainabilty and maintenece, the idea being that eventually this will be my main identity on the net, while keeping the few outlets of social media that I have at a safe social distance.

It's still a work in progress mind you as there's more artwork and animation projects that I need to upload, I also intend to add some form of a blog/journal to record my process and any additional thoughts.

So yeah, let's see how this turns out then.
